Smart Lights

Smart lighting is lighting that you can control from an app, usually on your Apple or Android phone. Smart bulbs can completely transform the way your home looks, for a lot less than it'd cost to redecorate.

QLED TV

QLED stands for Quantum Light-Emitting Diode. In non-geek-speak, that means a QLED TV is just like a regular LED TV, except it uses tiny nanoparticles called quantum dots to super-charge its brightness and color.

AirPods

AirPods Pro use the same H1 chip found in second generation AirPods, but add active noise cancellation, automated frequency profile setting, IPX4 water resistance, a charging case with wireless charging, and interchangeable silicone tips.
